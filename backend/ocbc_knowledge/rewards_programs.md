# OCBC Rewards Programs

## OCBC Rewards (Points System)

OCBC Rewards is the unified loyalty programme across OCBC credit cards, allowing cardholders to earn and redeem reward points for a wide range of perks.

### How to Earn Points

| Card Type | Local Spend | Overseas Spend |
|---|---|---|
| OCBC 365 Card | 0.3 reward points per S$1 | 0.6 reward points per S$1 |
| OCBC Frank Card | 0.2 reward points per S$1 | 0.4 reward points per S$1 |
| OCBC Premier Visa | 2 reward points per S$1 | 3 reward points per S$1 |
| NTUC Plus! Card | 1 LinkPoint per S$1 | 1 LinkPoint per S$1 |

### Points Validity
- Reward points are valid for 3 years from the date earned
- Points expire on a rolling basis
- Points from supplementary cards are combined with principal cardholder

### Bonus Points
- **New card sign-up**: Up to 10,000 bonus reward points
- **Birthday month**: 2x points on all spends (select cards)
- **Anniversary bonus**: 5,000 points for annual spend above S$20,000

## OCBC Voyage Miles

Voyage miles are a premium rewards currency offered to OCBC Voyage and Premier cardholders, redeemable for flights and upgrades.

### How to Earn Voyage Miles

| Card | Local Rate | Overseas Rate |
|---|---|---|
| OCBC Voyage Card | 2.3 miles per S$1 | 3.0 miles per S$1 |
| OCBC Premier Visa | 1.5 miles per S$1 | 2.0 miles per S$1 |

### Redemption Options

| redemption | Miles Required |
|---|---|
| Singapore Airlines economy ticket (Singapore–Bangkok) | 20,000 miles |
| Singapore Airlines economy ticket (Singapore–Tokyo) | 40,000 miles |
| Business class upgrade (Singapore–Hong Kong) | 30,000 miles |
| Hotel stay (1 night, 4-star) | 15,000 miles |

### Transfer Partners
- Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer (1:1 ratio)
- Cathay Pacific Asia Miles (1:1 ratio)
- Malaysia Airlines Enrich (1:1 ratio)
- British Airways Executive Club (1:1 ratio)

### Key Benefits
- Miles do not expire while card is active
- Complimentary lounge access (Voyage card)
- Travel insurance coverage included
- Priority check-in and boarding (Voyage card)

## OCBC Rewards Catalogue

The OCBC Rewards Catalogue allows cardholders to redeem points for merchandise, vouchers, and experiences.

### Redemption Categories

| Category | Examples | Points Range |
|---|---|---|
| Dining vouchers | GrabFood, Foodpanda, Starbucks | 2,000–10,000 points |
| Shopping vouchers | Shopee, Lazada, Amazon | 5,000–20,000 points |
| Travel | Airlines, hotels, experiences | 10,000–100,000 points |
| Electronics | AirPods, tablets, gadgets | 20,000–80,000 points |
| Charitable donations | Community Chest, WWF | Any amount |

### How to Redeem
1. Open OCBC Digital App
2. Navigate to "Rewards" section
3. Browse the catalogue
4. Select item and confirm redemption
5. Vouchers delivered instantly; physical items within 14 days

## NTUC LinkPoints (for NTUC Plus! Card)

### How to Earn
- 1% rebate on all spends (1 LinkPoint = S$0.01)
- 6% rebate at NTUC FairPrice, Unity, and Giant
- 4% rebate at all petrol stations

### Redemption
- 100 LinkPoints = S$1 discount at NTUC FairPrice
- Redeemable at NTUC FairPrice, Unity, Giant, and Guardian
- LinkPoints expire 12 months after being earned
