# OCBC Investment Products

## OCBC Securities

OCBC Securities provides access to a wide range of investment products across local and global markets.

### Markets Available
- **Singapore (SGX)**: Equities, ETFs, bonds, REITs
- **Hong Kong (HKEX)**: Equities, ETFs
- **United States (NYSE/NASDAQ)**: Equities, ETFs
- **United Kingdom (LSE)**: Equities
- **Australia (ASX)**: Equities

### Trading Fees (Online)

| Market | Commission | Minimum Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Singapore | 0.05% | S$10 |
| Hong Kong | 0.12% | HK$100 |
| United States | 0.08% | US$10 |
| Australia | 0.15% | A$15 |

- No custody fees for local securities
- Platform fee: S$0 for online trades
- CDP-linked account for SGX securities

### Key Features
- Online trading via OCBC Securities app and web platform
- Real-time market data and research reports
- IPO applications
- Margin financing available (up to 70% for SGX)
- Cash upfront account option

## OCBC Unit Trusts

OCBC offers a comprehensive range of unit trusts managed by leading fund houses globally.

### Fund Categories
- **Equity Funds**: Global, regional, and sector-specific equity funds
- **Bond Funds**: Government, corporate, and high-yield bond funds
- **Balanced Funds**: Multi-asset portfolios with varying risk profiles
- **Money Market Funds**: Low-risk, liquid funds for short-term parking
- **ESG Funds**: Sustainability-focused investment options

### Key Fund Platforms
- **OCBC Fund Selector**: Tool to compare and choose funds
- **Regular Investment Plan**: Invest from S$100/month via GIRO
- **Lump Sum**: Minimum S$1,000 per transaction

### Fees
- **Sales charge**: Up to 5% (varies by fund)
- **Management fee**: 0.5%–2.0% p.a. (fund dependent)
- **Switching fee**: S$0 for online switches between OCBC funds
- **No custody fee**

### Eligibility
- Age: 18 years and above
- Minimum investment: S$100 (regular), S$1,000 (lump sum)
- Available to Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and foreigners

## OCBC Robo-Advisory (Wealth Advisory)

OCBC's robo-advisory service provides automated portfolio management based on your risk profile and financial goals.

### How It Works
1. Complete a risk assessment questionnaire
2. Receive a recommended portfolio allocation
3. Automated rebalancing based on market conditions
4. Monitor performance via OCBC Digital App

### Portfolio Options

| Risk Profile | Equity Allocation | Target Return |
|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 20% | 3–5% p.a. |
| Balanced | 50% | 5–7% p.a. |
| Aggressive | 80% | 7–9% p.a. |

### Fees
- **Management fee**: 0.40%–0.80% p.a. (based on risk profile)
- **No sales charge**
- **No minimum investment**: Start from S$1

### Key Features
- AI-driven portfolio management
- Automatic dividend reinvestment
- Tax optimisation
- Available via OCBC Digital App
- Regular investment plans available

## OCBC Structured Deposits

Structured deposits offer potentially higher returns than regular fixed deposits, with returns linked to the performance of underlying assets.

### Product Types
- **Equity-Linked Deposits**: Returns linked to equity indices (e.g., STI, S&P 500)
- **FX-Linked Deposits**: Returns linked to currency exchange rates
- **Commodity-Linked Deposits**: Returns linked to gold or oil prices

### Typical Terms
- **Tenure**: 1 to 12 months
- **Minimum deposit**: S$10,000
- **Currency**: SGD, USD, AUD, EUR
- **Principal protection**: 100% if held to maturity (for capital-guaranteed structures)

### Returns
- Returns depend on underlying asset performance
- Potential returns range from 3% to 15% p.a. (depending on product and market)
- Capital at risk if early redemption or non-guaranteed structures

### Eligibility
- Age: 18 years and above
- Minimum deposit: S$10,000
- Available to Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and foreigners
- Risk disclosure document must be acknowledged
