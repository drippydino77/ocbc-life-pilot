# OCBC Savings Accounts

## OCBC 360 Account

The OCBC 360 Account is a high-yield savings account that rewards you for banking activities like crediting your salary, increasing your account balance, insuring with OCBC, and investing.

### Interest Rates (2025–2026)

| Balance Tier | Base Interest | Bonus Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| First S$10,000 | 0.05% p.a. | Base rate |
| Next S$90,000 | Up to 3.85% p.a. | With salary credit, spending, and insurance/investment |
| Next S$100,000 | Up to 4.05% p.a. | Full conditions met |

### Bonus Categories

- **Salary Credit**: +1.2% p.a. on first S$100,000 when you credit ≥S$1,600/month via GIRO or PayNow
- **Increased Balance**: +0.8% p.a. when you increase total account balance month-on-month
- **OCBC Credit Card Spending**: +0.6% p.a. when you spend ≥S$500/month on an eligible OCBC credit card
- **Insurance**: +1.2% p.a. on first S$100,000 with a new eligible OCBC insurance policy
- **Investment**: +1.2% p.a. on first S$100,000 with a new eligible OCBC investment

### Eligibility
- Age: 18 years and above
- Minimum balance: S$1,000 to earn bonus interest; S$30,000 fall-below fee waived
- Existing OCBC account holders or new-to-bank customers welcome

## OCBC Bonus+ Account

The Bonus+ Account is designed for customers who want a straightforward savings account with bonus interest for maintaining a growing balance.

### Interest Rates

- Base interest: 0.05% p.a.
- Bonus interest: Up to 2.50% p.a. for maintaining and increasing balance month-on-month
- Effective rate: Up to 2.55% p.a. on balances up to S$100,000

### Key Features
- No salary credit required
- Bonus interest earned by consistently increasing account balance
- Monthly bonus interest credited at end of month
- Minimum balance: S$1,000
- Fall-below fee: S$2/month if balance drops below S$1,000

### Eligibility
- Age: 18 years and above
- Singapore Citizen, Permanent Resident, or eligible foreigner
- Individual account only

## OCBC Frank Account

The Frank Account is a youth savings account targeted at customers aged 16 to 29, offering competitive rates with a digital-first approach.

### Interest Rates

- Base interest: 0.05% p.a.
- Bonus interest: Up to 3.50% p.a. with salary credit and card spending
- Total effective rate: Up to 3.55% p.a.

### Key Features
- Free OCBC Frank Debit Visa Card with customisable designs
- No minimum initial deposit
- Cashback on eligible spending via debit card
- App-based account management through OCBC Digital
- Bonus interest conditions: credit salary ≥S$1,600/month and spend ≥S$500 on OCBC card

### Eligibility
- Age: 16 to 29 years old
- Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident
- First account can be opened online via OCBC Digital App

## OCBC Keyfactor Account

The Keyfactor Account is designed for customers who want to earn higher interest by bundling multiple OCBC banking products.

### Interest Rates

- Base interest: 0.05% p.a.
- Bonus interest: Up to 3.00% p.a. with eligible OCBC home loan, credit card, and insurance

### Key Features
- Rewards customers who use multiple OCBC products
- Bonus interest tiers based on number of qualifying products held
- Compatible with OCBC 360 additional bonus categories
- Minimum balance: S$1,000

### Eligibility
- Age: 18 years and above
- Must hold qualifying OCBC home loan, insurance policy, or credit card
- Available to Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and eligible foreigners
