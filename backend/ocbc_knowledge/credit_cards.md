# OCBC Credit Cards

## OCBC 365 Card

The OCBC 365 Card is a cashback credit card designed for everyday spending, offering tiered cashback across multiple categories.

### Annual Fee
- S$192.60/year (first 2 years waived for new cardholders)
- Supplementary card: S$96.30/year

### Cashback Structure

| Spend Category | Cashback Rate | Monthly Cap |
|---|---|---|
| Dining & groceries | 3% | S$50 |
| Petrol (all stations) | 3% | S$50 |
| Utilities (SP Group) | 3% | S$30 |
| Online shopping | 3% | S$50 |
| All other spends | 0.3% | No cap |

- Minimum monthly spend of S$800 required to earn bonus cashback
- Cashback earned is capped at S$180/month

### Benefits
- Contactless payment (Visa payWave)
- 0% interest instalment plans at selected merchants
- OCBC Pay Anyone integration
- Card controls via OCBC Digital App

### Eligibility
- Age: 21 years and above
- Minimum income: S$30,000/year (Singapore Citizens/PRs)
- Foreigners: S$45,000/year

## OCBC Frank Card

The OCBC Frank Card is a no-annual-fee credit card aimed at young professionals and students.

### Annual Fee
- S$0 (no annual fee forever)

### Cashback & Rewards
- 1% cashback on all spends, no minimum spend
- Up to 8% cashback at selected merchants (e.g., Grab, Foodpanda, Shopee)
- No monthly cashback cap

### Benefits
- Zero annual fee
- Contactless payment (Visa payWave)
- Customisable card designs
- Digital-first with full app control
- 0% interest instalment plans

### Eligibility
- Age: 18 years and above
- Minimum income: S$24,000/year (Singapore Citizens/PRs)
- Students: Valid student pass with part-time income

## NTUC Plus! Card (OCBC Co-Branded)

The NTUC Plus! Card is a co-branded credit card between OCBC and NTUC FairPrice, offering enhanced rewards at NTUC merchants.

### Annual Fee
- S$168.50/year (waived for first year)
- Supplementary card: S$84.25/year

### Rewards Structure

| Spend Category | Rewards Rate |
|---|---|
| NTUC FairPrice / Unity / Giant | 6% LinkPoints rebate |
| Petrol (all stations) | 4% savings |
| All other spends | 1% LinkPoints rebate |

### Benefits
- LinkPoints earned on every transaction
- Exclusive NTUC FairPrice discounts and promotions
- Contactless payment
- Monthly cash rebates on NTUC spend
- Free supplementary card

### Eligibility
- Age: 21 years and above
- Minimum income: S$30,000/year (Singapore Citizens/PRs)
- NTUC membership recommended for full benefits

## OCBC Premier Visa Card

The OCBC Premier Visa Card is a premium credit card exclusive to OCBC Premier Banking customers.

### Annual Fee
- Complimentary for OCBC Premier Banking customers (minimum AUM S$200,000)

### Rewards & Benefits
- 2 reward points per S$1 spent locally
- 3 reward points per S$1 spent overseas
- Complimentary travel insurance (up to S$1,000,000)
- Priority access to OCBC Premier lounges
- Concierge service
- Airport limousine transfers (selected tiers)

### Key Features
- Visa Signature privileges
- Global acceptance
- Contactless payment
- Exclusive invitations to premium events

### Eligibility
- Must be an OCBC Premier Banking customer
- Minimum AUM: S$200,000
- By invitation or upon meeting Premier Banking criteria
