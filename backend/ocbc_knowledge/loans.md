# OCBC Loans

## OCBC Home Loan

OCBC offers competitive home loan packages for HDB flats, condominiums, and landed properties in Singapore.

### Interest Rate Packages (2025–2026)

| Package Type | Rate | Lock-in Period |
|---|---|---|
| Floating Rate (SORA-based) | 3.40% p.a. (Year 1), then SORA + 1.30% | 2–3 years |
| Fixed Rate | 3.25% p.a. (first 2 years), then浮动 | 2–3 years |
| Flexi Rate | SORA + 1.40% p.a. | None |

- SORA = Singapore Overnight Rate Average (replaces SIBOR)
- Rates are indicative and subject to change

### Loan Details
- **Loan-to-Value (LTV)**: Up to 75% for first property
- **Tenure**: Up to 30 years (HDB), up to 35 years (private property)
- **Minimum loan amount**: S$100,000
- **Early repayment penalty**: 1.5% of outstanding amount (during lock-in)

### Eligibility
- Age: 21 years and above
- Minimum income: S$24,000/year
- Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and foreigners with valid work passes
- Must meet MAS Total Debt Servicing Ratio (TDSR) of 55%

## OCBC Car Loan

OCBC provides vehicle financing for new and used cars purchased from authorised dealers.

### Interest Rates

| Loan Tenure | Interest Rate |
|---|---|
| 1–3 years | 2.68% p.a. (rest reducing) |
| 4–5 years | 2.88% p.a. (rest reducing) |
| 6–7 years | 3.18% p.a. (rest reducing) |

### Loan Details
- **Maximum financing**: Up to 70% of vehicle open market value (OMV)
- **Tenure**: 1 to 7 years
- **Minimum loan amount**: S$10,000
- **Processing fee**: S$200 (one-time)
- **Early redemption**: No penalty after lock-in period

### Eligibility
- Age: 21 years and above
- Minimum income: S$24,000/year
- Must be purchasing from authorised motor dealer
- Valid driving licence required

## OCBC Personal Loan

The OCBC Personal Loan provides quick access to funds for various personal needs, with flexible repayment options.

### Interest Rates

| Customer Type | Monthly Rate | Effective Rate |
|---|---|---|
| New-to-bank | 2.88% p.a. | 5.42% p.a. (EIR) |
| Existing OCBC customers | 3.18% p.a. | 5.99% p.a. (EIR) |
| Salary credit customers | 2.68% p.a. | 5.06% p.a. (EIR) |

### Loan Details
- **Loan amount**: S$500 to S$200,000
- **Tenure**: 12 to 60 months
- **Processing fee**: 1% of approved loan amount (min. S$100)
- **Disbursement**: Within 1 business day for existing customers
- **Early repayment**: 2% of outstanding balance or S$250 (whichever is higher)

### Eligibility
- Age: 21 to 65 years old
- Minimum income: S$20,000/year
- Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, or foreigners with S/E/P/Q work passes
- No minimum salary credit required, but lower rates for salary credited customers

## OCBC Education Loan

OCBC Education Loan helps finance local and overseas tertiary education for students and their sponsors.

### Interest Rates

| Study Location | Interest Rate |
|---|---|
| Local institutions | 4.50% p.a. (floating, pegged to SORA) |
| Overseas institutions | 4.80% p.a. (floating) |

### Loan Details
- **Loan amount**: Up to S$100,000 (local), up to S$150,000 (overseas)
- **Tenure**: Up to 10 years (repayment), up to 8 years (moratorium)
- **Moratorium period**: Up to 8 years (study period + 1 year grace)
- **Repayment**: Monthly instalments commence after moratorium
- **Processing fee**: S$100 (one-time)

### Eligibility
- Student must be enrolled in an accredited institution
- Sponsor must be 21 to 65 years old
- Minimum income for sponsor: S$24,000/year
- Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, or foreigners with valid passes
- Sponsor must be a blood relative or legal guardian
