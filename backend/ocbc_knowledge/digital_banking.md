# OCBC Digital Banking

## OCBC Digital App

The OCBC Digital App is a comprehensive mobile banking platform available on iOS and Android, offering full banking functionality on the go.

### Core Features
- **Account overview**: View all OCBC accounts, cards, and investments in one place
- **Real-time notifications**: Instant alerts for transactions and account activity
- **Biometric login**: Fingerprint and Face ID authentication
- **Card controls**: Lock/unlock cards, set spending limits, and manage overseas usage
- **Cheque imaging**: Deposit cheques by taking a photo
- **FD placement**: Open and manage fixed deposits directly in-app

### Bill Payment & Transfers
- **GIRO management**: Set up, modify, or cancel GIRO arrangements
- **PayNow**: Transfer funds instantly using mobile number, NRIC, or UEN
- **FAST transfers**: Real-time transfers to other Singapore banks
- **Overseas remittance**: Transfer to 200+ countries via Wise or traditional bank transfer
- **QR payments**: Scan and pay at merchants using SGQR code

### Investment & Insurance
- View and manage investment portfolio
- Purchase unit trusts and structured deposits
- Apply for insurance products
- Access research reports and market data

## PayNow

PayNow is a Singapore-wide service that enables instant fund transfers using a registered proxy (mobile number, NRIC, or UEN).

### How to Use PayNow with OCBC
1. **Register**: Link your mobile number or NRIC to your OCBC account via the app
2. **Transfer**: Enter the recipient's mobile number, NRIC, or UEN
3. **Confirm**: Verify the recipient's name and amount
4. **Receive**: Funds are credited instantly, 24/7

### PayNow Features
- **Transfer limit**: Up to S$100,000 per transaction (OCBC), S$5,000 for new registrations
- **24/7 availability**: Transfers work anytime, including weekends and holidays
- **Free transfers**: No fees for PayNow transfers via OCBC
- **Business payments**: Use UEN for business-to-business transfers
- **PayNow QR**: Scan merchant QR codes to pay for purchases

## OCBC Pay Anyone

OCBC Pay Anyone is a proprietary feature within the OCBC Digital App that allows quick person-to-person payments.

### Key Features
- **Contact sharing**: Send money via SMS, WhatsApp, or email links
- **Request payments**: Request money from friends with a push notification
- **Group splits**: Split bills among multiple people
- **QR code payments**: Generate and scan QR codes for instant transfers
- **Transaction history**: View all Pay Anyone transactions

### How It Works
1. Open OCBC Digital App and select "Pay Anyone"
2. Enter recipient's mobile number or email
3. Enter amount and add a reference note
4. Confirm with biometric or PIN authentication
5. Recipient receives notification and funds instantly (if registered) or via link

## Card Controls

OCBC provides robust card management features through the Digital App.

### Features
- **Instant lock/unlock**: Freeze and unfreeze your card in real-time
- **Transaction limits**: Set daily spending and ATM withdrawal limits
- **Overseas usage**: Enable or disable international transactions
- **Online purchases**: Toggle e-commerce transactions on/off
- **Merchant category blocks**: Block specific merchant types (e.g., gambling, adult content)
- **Virtual card**: Generate a virtual card number for online shopping
