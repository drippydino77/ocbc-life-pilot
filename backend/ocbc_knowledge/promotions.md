## Referral Programme

### General Referral Terms
- **Referrer reward**: S$50–S$150 (product dependent)
- **Referee reward**: S$50–S$100 (product dependent)
- **Referral limit**: Up to 10 referrals per calendar year
- **Processing time**: Rewards credited within 8 weeks of qualification

### How to Refer
1. Open OCBC Digital App
2. Navigate to "Refer a Friend" section
3. Share your unique referral link via SMS, WhatsApp, or email
4. Friend must apply and meet minimum requirements
5. Both parties receive rewards after qualification period
