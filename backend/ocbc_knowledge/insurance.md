# OCBC Insurance Products

## Life Insurance

OCBC Insurance offers a range of life insurance products to protect your family's financial future.

### OCBC Term Life Plus
- **Coverage**: Up to S$2,000,000
- **Premium**: From S$8/month (age 25, non-smoker)
- **Tenure**: 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, or 30 years
- **Death & TPD benefit**: Lump sum payout
- **Terminal illness benefit**: Accelerated payout
- **Waiver of premium**: On total and permanent disability

### OCBC Whole Life Plan
- **Coverage**: Up to S$1,000,000
- **Premium payment term**: 15, 20, or 25 years
- **Coverage term**: Lifetime
- **Cash value**: Guaranteed and non-guaranteed bonuses
- **Dividend payouts**: Annual cash dividends after Year 3

### Eligibility
- Age: 16 to 60 years old (at entry)
- Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and foreigners on valid passes
- Medical underwriting may be required for high-sum assured

## Health Insurance

### OCBC Health Shield GoldMax
- **Coverage**: Up to S$2,000,000 per policy year
- **Premium**: From S$180/year (age 25)
- **Hospital ward**: Class A/B1 (public) or private hospital
- **Pre-hospitalisation**: 90 days before admission
- **Post-hospitalisation**: 365 days after discharge
- **Day surgery**: Covered up to policy limit
- **Critical illness rider**: Available as add-on

### OCBC Critical Care
- **Coverage**: 37 critical illnesses
- **Lump sum payout**: Up to S$500,000 upon diagnosis
- **Premium**: From S$120/year (age 25)
- **Waiting period**: 90 days from policy start

### Eligibility
- Age: 15 days to 65 years old (at entry)
- Renewal guaranteed up to age 100
- No medical examination for sum assured up to S$300,000

## Car Insurance

### OCBC Car Insurance
- **Coverage types**: Comprehensive, Third Party Fire & Theft, Third Party Only
- **Premium**: From S$450/year (for a typical sedan, age 30)
- **Excess**: S$500 (standard), S$300 (young driver)
- **NCD protection**: Available as add-on

### Key Benefits
- **24/7 roadside assistance**
- **Courtesy car**: Up to 5 days during repairs
- **Windscreen cover**: Included in comprehensive plan
- **Personal accident**: Up to S$20,000 for driver and passengers
- **No-Claim Discount (NCD)**: Up to 50% for claim-free years

### Claims Process
1. Report accident via OCBC app or hotline (1800-222-1111)
2. Submit photos and documents online
3. Approve repair at panel workshop
4. Claim settled within 7 working days (simple claims)

### Eligibility
- Vehicle registered in Singapore
- Driver must hold valid Singapore driving licence
- Vehicle must be less than 15 years old for comprehensive cover

## Travel Insurance

### OCBC Travel Insurance (by NTUC Income)
- **Coverage types**: Basic, Standard, Premium
- **Premium**: From S$12 (ASEAN, 5 days)

### Coverage Summary

| Benefit | Basic | Standard | Premium |
|---|---|---|---|
| Medical expenses | S$100,000 | S$300,000 | S$500,000 |
| Personal accident | S$100,000 | S$200,000 | S$300,000 |
| Trip cancellation | S$3,000 | S$5,000 | S$10,000 |
| Baggage loss | S$1,000 | S$2,000 | S$3,000 |
| Travel delay | S$100/day | S$150/day | S$200/day |

### Key Features
- **COVID-19 coverage**: Included for medical expenses abroad
- **Adventure sports cover**: Included in Standard and Premium plans
- **Rental car excess**: Up to S$2,000 (Premium plan)
- **Automatic coverage**: When trip purchased with OCBC credit card
- **Family plan**: Up to 2 adults + 4 children under one policy

### Eligibility
- Age: 16 to 80 years old
- Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents
- Coverage worldwide (excluding sanctioned countries)
- Single trip or annual multi-trip options
